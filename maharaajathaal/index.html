Our team comprises of professionals who planted a promising concept of offering food lovers the authentic culture and taste of Rajasthani and Gujarati cuisine. First ever Maharaaja Thaal restaurant begun in 2013 at Belapur, followed with other two branches in Kharghar and Vashi in the year 2015.
