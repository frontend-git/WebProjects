ABOUT US

Started in 2015 at Vashi with a 60 person serving capacity, Maharaaja Thaal offered an experience of hospitality blended with delicious taste, excellent food quality and impeccable services, which would drag you to our restaurant. We have incorporated Indian values and traditions by retaining thali dining culture serving Rajasthani and Gujarati cuisine.

Our ample experience in restaurant industry, have made Maharaaja Thaal, a renowned name achieving peak position in the industry. Our forte is to serve delicious food satisfying taste buds of several food lovers. We are known for the hospitality and delicacies with pleasant heart and smiling face urging food lovers to enjoy the thali dining culture with sheer satisfaction. Considering TASTE and SERVICE the ultimate goal, we avoid compromising with its standards.
